1. Introduction

Welcome to "LifeChoice" (the "App"). This App is developed and operated by [Developer Name/Individual Developer] ("we" or "us"). We take your privacy seriously and comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Apple's App Store Review Guidelines. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information and the rights you have.

2. Definition of Personal Information

Personal Information means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, which can be used alone or in combination with other information to identify an individual.

3. Information Collection and Use

3.1 Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • No collection of personal basic information such as name, email, phone number
  • No collection of device identifiers (IDFA, UUID, IMEI, MEID, MAC address, etc.)
  • No collection of location information
  • No collection of usage behavior data
  • No collection of browsing history, search records, etc.
  • No collection of biometric information
  • No collection of sensitive personal information such as financial information or health information

3.2 Local Data Storage

All data in the App is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Items you add (names, images)
  • Categories and scenarios you create
  • Random selection history
  • App settings (such as language preferences)

This data remains on your device only and is never uploaded to any server, nor accessed by us or any third party.

3.3 Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union or the United Kingdom, under GDPR, we declare:

  • Since this App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, we do not conduct data processing activities that require a legal basis
  • All data is stored locally on your device and is fully controlled by you

3.4 No Sale of Personal Information (CCPA/CPRA)

For users in California, United States, under CCPA/CPRA, we declare:

  • This App does not collect any personal information, so we do not sell your personal information
  • You do not need to exercise the "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" option because we have no personal information to sell

4. Data Access and Control

  • Third parties (including the app developer) cannot access your locally stored data
  • You can view, modify, or delete locally stored data at any time
  • You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect locally stored data:

  • The App uses secure local storage mechanisms
  • Data is only used within your device

We also recommend that you:

  • Regularly back up important data
  • Export important content before uninstalling
  • Use device passcode/biometric authentication
  • Download apps from official channels

6. User Rights

In accordance with applicable laws and regulations, including the Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), you have the following rights:

6.1 General Rights

  • Access Right: You can access all data stored on your device at any time
  • Correction Right: You can correct all data stored on your device at any time
  • Deletion Right: You can delete any data stored on your device at any time
  • Data Portability Right: You can export data to other apps (if applicable)

6.2 Special Rights for EU/UK Users (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Union or the United Kingdom, under GDPR, you also have the following rights:

  • Right to Restrict Processing: In certain circumstances, you have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data based on legitimate interests or public interest
  • Right to Object to Automated Decision-Making: You have the right to object to decisions based solely on automated processing (including profiling)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If the processing of your personal data is based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time

6.3 Special Rights for California Users (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a resident of California, under CCPA/CPRA, you also have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, and share about you
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information that we have collected
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: You have the right to request that we do not sell your personal information (this App does not collect or sell personal information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You will not be discriminated against for exercising your rights under CCPA/CPRA

6.4 Exercising Your Rights

  • Since all data in this App is stored locally on your device, you can exercise the above rights directly through the App interface
  • If you need our assistance or have any questions, please contact us through the contact information provided in Section 10
  • We will respond to your request within a reasonable time (not exceeding the period specified by applicable law)

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 13, please use the App with the guidance of your parents or guardians.

These services may have their own privacy policies, but we do not collect your personal information through these services. You can review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand their information processing practices.

For users in the European Union or the United Kingdom, under GDPR, if a security breach occurs that is likely to affect the security of your personal data, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of discovering the breach. Since all data in this App is stored locally on your device, the data retention period is determined by you. You can delete any data stored on your device at any time, or delete all data by uninstalling the App.

11. Data Transfer (GDPR)

For users in the European Union or the United Kingdom, under GDPR, if you need to transfer your personal data to another service provider, you can export the data through the App interface. Since this App does not collect or store any personal data, data transfer is completely controlled by you.

12. Additional Rights for California Users (CCPA/CPRA)

For users in California, United States, under CCPA/CPRA, you also have the right to:

Since this App does not collect or store any personal information, you do not need to exercise these rights.
